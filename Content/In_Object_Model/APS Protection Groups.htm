APS/MSP Protection Groups

APS/MSP Protection Groups provide a method of establishing port level protection for the client ports of a circuit.  Information about a network's APS/MSP Protection Groups can be imported into the Modeler for viewing and reporting purposes.

The following applies to APS/MSP Protection Groups in the Modeler:

  • APS/MSP Protection Groups appear in the object tree as a child object of a location and are symbolized by this icon: .
  • APS/MSPs cannot be created or configured within the Modeler.  They are configured within nd  become part of a network file when network information is imported into the Modeler. 
  • APS/MSP Protection Groups are not depicted in network views.
  • The properties of APS/MSP objects are read-only.
