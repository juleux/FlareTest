Using the Pan and Zoom Window

The Pan and Zoom window is a tool to aid you in navigating between different areas within a network view.  To open the Pan and Zoom window, click on the icon in the upper-left corner of the network view you wish to edit.  The window contains a miniaturized schematic of the view being edited.  By default, the entire network view is selected for display.  To select a portion of the view to zoom in on, click and drag (within the pan and zoom window) to form a rectangular selection box (rubberband) around the desired area.  The network view will be immediately updated with your selection as shown below.

Pan and Zoom Window - Zoom

Panning is enabled only when the entire view is not visible.  To pan within a view:

  1. Click and drag (within the pan and zoom window) to form a rectangular selection box (rubberband) of the desired size.
  2. Position the cursor on one of the edges of the selection box.  The cursor will change as shown below.  
  3. Click and hold down the left mouse key.  
  4. Drag the selection box within the pan window.  The view will be updated as you pan.

Pan and Zoom Window - Pan

To close the Pan and Zoom window, click on the icon in the upper-left corner.
